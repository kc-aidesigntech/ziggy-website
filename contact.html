Frequently Asked Questions

How does Ziggy work?

+

Ziggy is a smart communication device that connects to your home WiFi. Parents use our companion app to manage approved contacts and call times. Kids can then make voice calls to approved contacts without any apps, games, or internet distractions.

Is Ziggy safe for kids?

+

Absolutely! Ziggy is designed with child safety as the top priority. Only parent-approved contacts can call your child, and you can set quiet hours and Do Not Disturb times. There are no apps, games, or internet browsing capabilities.

What's the difference between Ziggy and Ziggy Flashback?

+

Both devices have the same core functionality and safety features. Ziggy has a modern design, while Ziggy Flashback has a retro-inspired look that parents will remember from their childhood. The choice is purely aesthetic!

Do I need the Family Plan to use Ziggy?

+

No! Ziggy-to-Ziggy calling is always free. The Family Plan ($9.99/month) is only needed if you want your child to call regular phone numbers like grandparents' landlines or parents' cell phones.

When will Ziggy be available?

+

We're currently in the final development phase and expect to begin shipping in Q2 2024. Pre-orders are open now, and you'll receive regular updates on our progress.
