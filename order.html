Frequently Asked Questions

When will my Ziggy ship?

Pre-orders are currently being accepted. We expect to begin shipping in Q2 2024. You'll receive email updates as we get closer to the launch date.

What's included in the box?

Each Ziggy comes with the device, power adapter, setup instructions, and access to our parent companion app.

Do I need a subscription?

Ziggy-to-Ziggy calling is always free. The Family Plan ($9.99/month) is optional and only needed if you want to call regular phone numbers.

Is there a warranty?

Yes! All Ziggy devices come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship.
